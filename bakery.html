About Us

Our Background

Patisserie is owned by Jamie and Chung who have been operating the beloved Patisserie for over 12 years. For Jamie, being a pastry chef is in her genes. She grew up in the family business in Paris, where her parents, grandparents, and brother were all pastry chefs. She came to the Thailand knowing she would carry on the family tradition. Now a staple in community, Pastisserie is enjoyed by the entire Bangkok community. Often selling out their luxurious sweets and pastry on weekdays, Locals know that a trip to Pastiesserie is a wonderful way to make your day.
